PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

It is joint venture project between Goel Ganga Developments, Gagan Developers, Kalyanee Constructions and Fortune Properties. Ganga Acropolis comprises exclusive 6 high-rise towers, offering spectacular 2 bedroom residences. Located in Baner-Sus, each residence enjoys a breath-taking view of the cityscape along with a blissful harmony of natural beauty. Coupled with stunning architecture and innovative design, Acropolis makes a striking statement of modernity and holds an unrivalled sense of excellence.
